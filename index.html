There have been 45 presidents in the history of the United States.

The first president, George Washington served in the 1700's. He was was an American soldier, statesman, and Founding Father of the USA.

The 11th president James Knox Polk. He was the 13th Speaker of the House of Representatives and ninth governor of Tennessee. He served in the 1800's.

The 35th President of the United States was John Fitzgerald Kennedy. Serving in the 1900's, he was the youngest man elected to the office and unfortunately the youngest to die in office as well.

The 44th president of the United States was Barack Hussein Obama II in the 2000's. He was a member of the Democratic Party, Obama was the first African-American president of the United States.
